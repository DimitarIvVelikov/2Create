What We Live By

Service With a Smile

Our employees are known for their great work ethic, politeness, and overall enthusiastic approach. Our strict uniform of steel toed boots and proper equipment on the job site ensures we are offering a personable and professional experience.

Helping The Earth

We recycle everything we can, from cardboard to that old tire. With companies like Value Village and foundations like Salvation Army we are able to donate or recycle an estimated 20-50% of the junk we take away. Everything that can't be donated or recycled goes to Bailey's Sanitary Landﬁll.

Transparent Pricing

Our rates are based our rates off of estimated dump fees. We also take into consideration other factors, such as labour costs and gas. Our quotes include everything, giving you an "All In" estimate.
